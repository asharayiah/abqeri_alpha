Safe-AI — Policy Brief

← Back to Home  ·  Open Safe-AI

Abqeri Safe-AI is designed to keep people safe while keeping AI useful. It pairs **clear guardrails** with **transparent explanations** and can run in **Hybrid mode** where safety and provenance are enforced by our hardware path when available.

Guardrails Interpretability Provenance Hybrid (Hardware-backed) Low-cost, accessible

What makes it safe?

Explicit content policies
Requests linked to violence, self-harm, exploitation, or illegal acts are **detected** and **refused** with a humane explanation and alternatives.
Guardrail transparency
When a safety rule triggers, the UI shows a labeled reason (e.g., “Violent wrongdoing detected”) so users and auditors see **why**.
Streaming + audit trail
Answers stream token-by-token, accompanied by a policy “meta” record (model, mode, guardrail IDs). Logs can be exported if required.
Hybrid provenance
When the FPGA channel is connected, the system flips to **Hybrid (Hardware-backed)** mode. The provenance strip shows the active path.

Why it’s different

  • Interpretability for users: not just “blocked” — we explain the reason and offer safe alternatives.
  • Accessible safety: runs in software today, and becomes hardware-verified when the FPGA is available.
  • Inclusive by design: the interface and content localize to EN / AR / RU / ZH and respect RTL.

Deployment options

Software fallback
Runs on Cloudflare Workers AI with the same policy engine and explanations, suitable for pilots now.
Hybrid hardware
When BLE/Serial detects our FPGA, safety + provenance are hardware-backed. The compute path and badges update live in the UI.
Data protection
Edge execution; no training on user inputs; optional redaction for PII before inference.

What end-users experience

  • **Clear, respectful refusals** when harm is detected, with suggested safe directions.
  • **Gold provenance strip** indicating model and compute path (Software vs Hybrid).
  • **Enter-to-send** chat, RTL support, and streaming output.
